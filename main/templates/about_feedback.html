ABOUT US

Crusher Fantasy is a Dynasty Basketball Companion tool. With the idea to be able to take something complex such as Dynasty Basketball and provide tools to make it easier for the player. At the moment the only feature is the crowd-sourced rankings. We take all the data from individual players voting to make an accurate ranking of all the NBA Players to help managers analysis trades, waivers and drafts. In the future we plan to implement a Trade Calculator, Start/Sit tool, Expert Rankings, Team Analysis, Mock Draft tool, and Prospect Rankings.
