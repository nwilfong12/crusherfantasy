About Crusher Fantasy

Crusher Fantasy is a dynasty basketball companion platform built to simplify complex decision-making for fantasy managers. Our current feature uses crowd-sourced voting to generate real-time dynasty rankings across the NBA. By aggregating thousands of individual evaluations, we aim to provide a more accurate view of player value to help managers make smarter decisions in trades, waiver moves, drafts, and long-term roster construction.

Future tools will include a Trade Calculator, Start/Sit Optimizer, Expert Rankings integration, Team Analysis dashboards, Mock Draft simulations, and Prospect scouting rankings.
